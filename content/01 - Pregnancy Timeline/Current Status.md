## 🎯 Current Action Items & Focus
*Highlight the items corresponding to your current trimester.*

### First Trimester (Weeks 1–13)
- [ ] Daily prenatal vitamin (folic acid, DHA, choline)
- [ ] Establish care with OB/GYN or Midwife
- [ ] Confirm first appointment date and dating ultrasound
- [ ] Research and decide on optional NIPT / genetic screening
- [ ] Manage morning sickness, fatigue, and physical changes

### Second Trimester (Weeks 14–27)
- [ ] Schedule and complete 20-week Anatomy Scan
- [ ] Register for birth hospital or birth center
- [ ] Schedule and complete Glucose Challenge Test (GCT) (Weeks 24–28)
- [ ] Start compiling baby registry and researching gear
- [ ] Begin pelvic floor and core conditioning exercises

### Third Trimester (Weeks 28–40+)
- [ ] Complete Tdap vaccination (Weeks 27–36)
- [ ] Take childbirth, infant care, and breastfeeding classes
- [ ] Draft and discuss Birth Plan with your provider (complete by Week 36)
- [ ] Complete Group B Strep (GBS) screening (Weeks 35–37)
- [ ] Pack Hospital Bag for birth person, partner, and baby (complete by Week 36–37)
- [ ] Install baby car seat and get it safety inspected (complete by Week 36)
